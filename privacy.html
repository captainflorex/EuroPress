EuroPress Privacy Policy

Learn how we protect your data and respect your rights. Last updated: December 8, 2025.

Introduction

At EuroPress, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, EuroPress.com.

Information We Collect

We collect two main types of information:

  • Personal Information (Direct): Data you voluntarily provide, such as your email address when you sign up for our newsletter. We do not collect your name or other contact details unless explicitly provided for a specific service (like a contact form).
  • Usage Data (Indirect): Information automatically collected as you interact with the site, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, and referring links. This is typically collected via cookies and analytics services.
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to improve site functionality, remember preferences, and analyze traffic. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the website, ensuring all features function correctly.
  • To improve site content and user experience by analyzing traffic and usage patterns.
  • To send the EuroPress newsletter, if you have specifically subscribed.
  • To detect and prevent technical issues or security breaches.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your Personal Information to third parties. We may share Usage Data with trusted third-party services, such as Google Analytics or advertising partners, to analyze traffic and serve relevant ads. These third parties are bound by their own privacy policies.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Opt-out of receiving promotional emails by using the unsubscribe link in the newsletter.
  • Disable cookies in your browser settings to prevent the collection of Usage Data.
  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you.
